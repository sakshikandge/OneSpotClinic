A book review serves the purpose of helping others to decide if they should or want to read particular book or not. This is done by readingand evaluating a given book, and summing it up in terms of how well it has been written and/orhowwell the author has handled the subject matter. A review can explore the positive ornegative elements (or both) of a book.
