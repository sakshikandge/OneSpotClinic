About author

AUTHOR:-

Durjoy Datta (born February 7, 1987), aka BENGALI BABU, is an Indian author who writes for young adults. He graduated from Delhi College of Engineering and then went on to do PGDBM in marketing from Management Development Institute. He has co-founded Grapevine India Publishers. He is the author of 6 runaway bestselling books.

Brooke and Kennedy had two best friends in common. When they are forced together in a blizzard they finally make a start at becoming friends. Kennedy has a big family with five younger siblings and loving parents. Brooke never knew her father and her mother hated her. She grew up along. Can two worlds coalesce or will they collide? A slow burn romance. Happy reading!!
