Novel:- That Night

About author

AUTHOR:- NIDHI UPADHYAY

Nidhi is the bestselling author of That Night. She is an engineer, a headhunter, and a consumer of black coffee and dark chocolates. But her greatest interests are her children, her husband who is disillusioned that her writing career is his retirement plan, and a puppy who thinks he owns her. She is often found hiding her current search history from her boys. If not busy researching a way to kill, drown or dispose of bodies, Nidhi can be found reading thrillers or screaming at her boys..

What happence when an innocent prank goes horribaly wrong?
